Fred Smith resides in Long Island, New York and has been a freelance photographer for over 20 years.

Fred's experience ranges from shooting nature photographs for various magazines, to model photo shoots for catalogs to sports photography.

Through the use of different programs such as Photoshop, Fred is able to take his digital photos and enhance their best qualities to produce a final, quality image suitable for print, web or video usage.
